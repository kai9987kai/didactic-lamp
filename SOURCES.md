## Thermodynamics of learning and stochastic optimization

1. **Goldt, S., & Seifert, U. (2017). _Stochastic Thermodynamics of Learning_.**
   - arXiv: [1703.09247](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.09247)
   - Introduces thermodynamic bounds on information acquisition during learning.

2. **Chaudhari, P., & Soatto, S. (2018). _Stochastic Gradient Descent Performs Variational Inference, Converges to Limit Cycles for Deep Networks_.**
   - arXiv: [1710.11029](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.11029)
   - Connects SGD dynamics to stochastic differential equations and non-equilibrium interpretations.

3. **Mandt, S., Hoffman, M. D., & Blei, D. M. (2017). _Stochastic Gradient Descent as Approximate Bayesian Inference_.**
